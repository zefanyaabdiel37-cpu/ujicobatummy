Programs

Bagian ini berisi daftar layanan yang ditawarkan oleh Happy Tummy Kids. Tujuannya biar orang tua tahu jenis program apa saja yang tersedia, lengkap dengan kegiatan dan manfaatnya.

a. Full Day Care

  • Durasi: 08.00 – 17.00
  • Kegiatan belajar ringan (warna, angka, bentuk)
  • Arts & crafts
  • Storytelling
  • Nike Community Day: A day of fun activities, giveaways, and fitness challenges. Date: April 22, 2023.

a. Full Day Care

  • Nike Run Club Meet-Up: Join us every Saturday morning for a community run. All levels are welcome!
  • Women’s Empowerment Workshop: A special event focused on empowering women through sports and fitness. Date: March 15, 2023.
  • Local Sports Clinics: Free clinics for youth athletes to learn from professional coaches. Various dates throughout the month.
  • Nike Community Day: A day of fun activities, giveaways, and fitness challenges. Date: April 22, 2023.
